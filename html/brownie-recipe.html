Brownie

The perfect recipe for rich, chocolately and fudgy brownies.

Indulge in the ultimate chocolate treat with this rich and fudgy brownie recipe! Perfectly chewy on the edges and irresistibly gooey in the center, these homemade brownies are a chocolate lover's dream come true. Whether you're baking for a special occasion or just because, this simple recipe will have you enjoying delicious, bakery-style brownies right at home.

Get ready to bring pure chocolate bliss to your kitchen!

Ingredients

Lasagna

Prep Time:
15 mins

Cook Time:
30 mins

Total Time:
45 mins

Serving Size:
9 persons
Ingredients
Black chocolate - 240g Vanilla extract - 2 tsp
Cocoa powder - 75g Salt - 2 pinches
Butter - 280g Eggs - 6
Sugar - 400g Flour - 120g
Brown sugar - 100g  

Preparation

  1. Melt the butter. In the meantime cut the chocolate finely and add it in a bowl with 25g of cocoa powder. Add the melted butter and wisk until smooth
  2. In a larger bowl add all the sugar, the vanilla extract, the salt, one egg and mix. Then add 2 more eggs and mix. At last add the remaining eggs and mix for a few minutes.
  3. Add the chocolate-butter mixture and mix. Now add the flour and 50g of cocoa powder and mix the batter lightly with a spatula.
  4. Lastly, pop the mixture in the oven at 180°C for about 35 minutes for a fudgy consistency.
