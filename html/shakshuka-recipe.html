Shakshuka

Very fast, very nutritious and very easy to make

Bring a taste of the Mediterranean to your kitchen with this vibrant shakshuka recipe! A delightful dish of poached eggs nestled in a spicy, savory tomato and pepper sauce, shakshuka is perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even dinner. Bursting with flavor and easy to make in just one pan, this warm, comforting meal pairs wonderfully with fresh bread for dipping. Follow along for a simple recipe thatâ€™s sure to become a favorite!

  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Onion - 1
  • Bell pepper - 1
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Zucchini / Eggplant - 1
  • Mushrooms - 6
  • Tomato sauce - 350g
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chilli flakes
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  1. Cut all the vegetables.
  2. Saute the onion, bell pepper, and garlic with a little bit of extra virgin olive oil.
  3. Once softened, add the mushrooms and zucchini/eggplant and continue stirring.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and the aromatics and let it simmer for about 5 minutes..
  5. When the sauce is ready, use the back of a spoon and make some holes in the sauce.
  6. Crack your eggs and nestle each egg in one of the holes you created. Cover the skillet and allow the eggs to simmer in the sauce over medium-low heat until the egg whites have settled.
