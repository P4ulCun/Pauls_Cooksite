Shakshuka

Very fast, very nutritious and very easy to make

Bring a taste of the Mediterranean to your kitchen with this vibrant shakshuka recipe! A delightful dish of poached eggs nestled in a spicy, savory tomato and pepper sauce, shakshuka is perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even dinner. Bursting with flavor and easy to make in just one pan, this warm, comforting meal pairs wonderfully with fresh bread for dipping.

Follow along for a simple recipe that's sure to become a favorite!

Ingredients

Lasagna

Prep Time:
30 mins

Cook Time:
1 hour

Total Time:
1 hour 30 mins

Serving Size:
8 persons
Ingredients
Olive oil - 1 tablespoon Tomato sauce - 350g
Onion - 1 Salt
Bell pepper - 1 Pepper
Chilli pepper - 1 Cumin
Garlic - 3 cloves Basil
Zucchini / Eggplant - 1 Eggs - 3

Preparation

  1. Cut all the vegetables.
  2. Saute the onion, the peppers, and garlic with a little bit of extra virgin olive oil.
  3. Once softened, add the mushrooms and zucchini/eggplant and continue stirring.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and the aromatics and let it simmer for about 5 minutes..
  5. When the sauce is ready, use the back of a spoon and make some holes in the sauce.
  6. Crack your eggs and nestle each egg in one of the holes you created. Cover the skillet and allow the eggs to simmer in the sauce over medium-low heat until the egg whites have settled.
