Lasagna

An easy and delicious lasagna recipe perfect in the winter time

Get ready to savor the comforting layers of homemade lasagna! This classic Italian favorite combines tender pasta, rich tomato sauce and melted cheese into a deliciously hearty meal. Perfect for family gatherings or simply satisfying a craving, our easy-to-follow lasagna recipe ensures you’ll have a beautifully layered dish packed with flavor. Follow along for a step-by-step guide to creating this crowd-pleasing masterpiece right in your own kitchen!

  • Ground beef - 1kg
  • Tomato sauce - 1kg
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Onion - 1
  • Carrot - 2
  • Butter - 70g
  • Flour - 70g
  • Milk - 700ml
  • Lasagna sheets
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • Chicken bouillon
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Rosemary
  • Chilli flakes
  1. Heat up a Dutch oven pot with a thin oil film. Brown the meat thoroughly then take it out of the pot and sauté the cubed vegetables. In the same pot fry a bit of tomato paste then add the cooked meat and all the seasoning to taste and simmer for 1 hour
  2. In the meantime melt the butter in a pot. Then add the flour wisking vigorously until a paste is formed. Slowly add milk, again, wisking vigorously and some salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Now we need to assemble the lasagna. In a baking dish put a thin layer of the Bolognese sauce,then a layer of the pasta sheets, followed by a layer of Bechamel, then again a layer of Bolognese and repeat. Now finish with some grated mozzarella.
  4. Place in the oven at 180°C for about 25 minutes to brown the top.
